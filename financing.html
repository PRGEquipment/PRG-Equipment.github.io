Financing

Options

Cash Payment

Paying cash is much easier and costs less without interest costs and finance fees. Paying cash means that you won't have to make monthly payments. However, paying cash may take a large amount out of the current budget.

Leasing

There are many leasing options. Leasing allows you to have a forklift that is made for the task at hand. It is cheaper in the short run, but more expensive in the long run. For more specific options, please contact us so that we can work with you to make a customized solution.

Financing

Financing allows you to make monthly payments for a set number of months. When all the paymens are completed, you own the forklift. This is beneficial for people who expect to make enough money to buy the forklift eventually, but currently do not possess enough funds to buy a forklift.
